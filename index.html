Navigating Hypermedia
This is an isolated Hypertext Wiki that details the conceptual precursors of hypertext to look beyond the idea that hypermedia is a recent invention. Hyperlinking is a format of thinking rather than a technology. It is a nonlinear network of nodes and connective channels that mirrors the way our brain uses association to form thought. The prefix hyper typically means excess, over, or above. In the context of hypertext and hyperspace in theoretical physics, it often means something contained within another. Hyperspace can have a portal from one dimension to another so that you can seamlessly travel between. Hypertext can also be seen as a portal, where two distant points are brought closer and closer together to become one— the link.
